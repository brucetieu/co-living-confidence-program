You don't have a tenant problem.
You have a systems problem.

Most co-living operators spend weeks chasing leads, fielding bad-fit inquiries, and watching rooms sit empty. The issue isn't demand — it's the absence of repeatable systems for filtering tenants, writing listings that convert, and running showings that close. This workshop gives you all three.

01

The Qualifier™

Stop wasting time on bad-fit tenants. A systematic filtering framework that identifies high-quality candidates before you ever schedule a showing — so you only spend time on people who'll actually sign.

02

The Tenant Magnet™

Your listing is your 24/7 salesperson. Learn how to write and structure co-living listings that attract qualified tenants and repel the wrong ones — reducing noise and increasing conversion.

03

The Showing Script™

Showings aren't tours — they're interviews. A structured conversation framework that builds tenant confidence while giving you the signal to make fast, accurate yes/no decisions.
